# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use our Sudoku mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect
When you play our game, we automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with the App using **Google Analytics for Firebase**. This includes:
* **Gameplay Data:** Level starts, level completions (including time spent and stars earned), and use of features like hints or undos.
* **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
* **Usage Data:** App errors, navigation patterns, and how long you play specific levels.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
* Analyze game performance and improve level difficulty.
* Fix bugs and technical errors.
* Understand user engagement to provide a better gaming experience.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use **Firebase (Google)** to help us track analytics. Firebase may collect data as governed by their own privacy policy. You can find more information here: [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 4. Data Retention
We retain the collected analytics data for as long as necessary to provide insights into our game's performance.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Our App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
